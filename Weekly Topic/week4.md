## Week 4 Summary: Software Design (Design Patterns & Refactoring)

The goal for this week is to understand core software design concepts using materials from **ASE 420**, with a primary focus on **Design Patterns** and **Refactoring**.

---

## Core Discussion Topics

### 1. APIEC Principles
This section covers foundational object-oriented and development concepts:
* Abstraction & Interface
* Encapsulation & Dependency Injection (DI)
* Polymorphism & Composition
* Requirements & Use Case Diagrams
* Software Testing

### 2. SOLID Principles
A review of the five key principles of SOLID design:
* **SO:** Single Responsibility & Open/Closed Principles
* **LID:** Liskov Substitution, Interface Segregation, & Dependency Inversion Principles

### 3. Design Patterns (GoF)
The discussion introduces the core ideas of the Gang of Four (GoF) Design Patterns, using the **Iterator** pattern as a detailed example. There is no formal homework for this section, but students are encouraged to answer the questions in the provided slides.
